How U'Qube Works

Five simple steps from group creation to your first payout. Every member knows exactly what happens next.

01

Create Your Group

Name your group and invite members via email. No commitment yet â€” just bring your circle together.

02

Set the Rules

Once everyone joins, agree on a monthly contribution amount and cycle length as a group.

03

Spin the Wheel

The Wheel of Names spins once to set the payout order for the entire rotation. Fair, random, final.

04

Activate with Deposit

Each member pays a refundable deposit equal to one month's contribution to activate the group.

05

Contribute & Rotate

Monthly contributions are pooled and sent to the next person in order. Every member gets their turn.
